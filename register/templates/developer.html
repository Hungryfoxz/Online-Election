hdr_weak Background

I am from Sivasagar,Assam. I started my b.Tech journey from Jorhat Institute of Science & Technology along with a BCA degree parallely, both of which started in the same year i.e 2019. I am a Cybersecurity Enthusiast. But I also developed a taste in programming and as a result of which I started learning web Development during the period of lockdown due to Covid-19.

hdr_strong My Interest

As you have already known how i started learning web Development, You must also know that doing web Development is not my first hobby.The way I would describe it is - To break things the way they were made, you must first know how to make things. That is why web development opens a new wondow for me to my security perspective to be visualized in a extensible manner.

hdr_weak Idea of this Website

Now coming to the development of this website, which was total coincidence with the fact that, I was learning Full stack development during those days. And to my surprise I found a popup in my whatsapp, I opened it and saw that JIST was organizing a software development competition. Like the whole universe was telling me to do it. So I jumped right on to it. Although I had exams during that period but I couldn't think of anything else other than laying ideas on my head about how to make it.

hdr_strongProcess of Creation

So after having gone through a ton of ideas on the drawing board, it was time to actually implement it in such a way that it suits the need of the institution and also should be the one among various others as it was a part of a competition. I decided to go with Python to create the backend, and for that i had to take the help of the Django framework which was perfect for this scenario. Also one advantage with django is that it comes with an inbuilt SqlLite database, hence we are relieved from setting up our own database. I was familiar with frontend already. Eventhough I had worked with bootstrap but for this one I choose to go with MaterializeCSS. And with all that I created this Website.

hdr_weak Outcome

I would say I am pretty much happy with how the website looks like after many sleepless nights of hard work. I know its not perfect, but to me its more than what I imagined, of what I could achieve. It has truly redefined new boundaries to what I can do to what I could think of. My idea of participating in this competition was never about winnig, rather I saw this as an opportunity to grow myself. And it has really helped me to improve my skills.

hdr_strongFinal Message

Now I can say that hardwork really pays off. Why I am telling you this is because there were moments when I wanted to quit and pull myself off of this project. But I fought through that because something inside of me always told that I was better than that and in that moment of time I could see the ray of hope and found the courage to get back again.
